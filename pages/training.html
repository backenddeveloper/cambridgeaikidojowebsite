What to wear

If you have have Keikogi please feel free to wear it. Otherwise please wear comfortable loose-fitting clothing that you don't mind rolling in, such as T-shirt (long sleeve better than short sleeve) and track-suit trousers. Please avoid zippers, buttons, belts, watches and jewellery. Please note that Hakama are normally reserved for Yudansha.
